Soon to be launched in Stamford, Connecticut, the DCG Institute at UConn is a first-of-its-kind academic and research institute focused on cutting-edge blockchain/fintech technology and education.

The Institute represents a unique partnership between Digital Currency Group (DCG), the epicenter of the cryptocurrency industry, and the University of Connecticut (UConn), the top-ranked public university in New England.
